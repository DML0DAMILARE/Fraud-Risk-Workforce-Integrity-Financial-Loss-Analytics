Joint Model — Employee Fraud Risk Score

EFRS Model

The Employee Fraud Risk Score blends seven weighted risk factors into a single 0–100 score, banding every employee from Low to Critical risk.

EFRS = 0.30·Fraud Incident + 0.15·Suspicious Txn + 0.15·Expense Fraud + 0.10·Payroll Abuse + 0.10·Attendance Abuse + 0.10·Disciplinary History + 0.10·Performance Anomaly
